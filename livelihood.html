Livelihood

We believe in helping individuals live a life of dignity. To achieve this, our livelihood programmes focuses on generating sustainable livelihoods. This is done through capacity building, fostering community links and promoting small businesses. KASTURY In puts special focus on entrepreneurial ventures, which are owned by women. KASTURY through its livelihood programmes promotes microfinance, Self Help Groups, capacity building and small businesses.
